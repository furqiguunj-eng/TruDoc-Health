02 — Voice Framework

The Four Pillars

Our voice is constant: the ultimate medical authority — evidence-based, trustworthy, completely transparent. These pillars define every word we write and say.

01
Authoritative,
Yet Empathetic

We deal with people's health and their lives. The voice must immediately establish the patient is in safe, highly capable hands — without sounding cold or clinical.

DO Use calm, reassuring language. Acknowledge the patient's situation.
DON'T Sound robotic, dismissive, or use generic call-center scripts.
In practice "We will have a doctor call you back within 30 minutes." — not "Your call is important to us, please hold."
02
Transparent
& Direct

Our biggest differentiator is that we practice evidence-based medicine and tell patients the truth about what they need. We do not hide behind complex jargon.

DO Get straight to the point. Be brutally honest about wait times, costs, and medical realities.
DON'T Use corporate healthcare buzzwords or obscure facts to sound softer.
In practice "Based on NHS guidelines, this requires an at-home lab test." — not "We suggest exploring comprehensive diagnostic modalities."
03
Accessible
& Inclusive

We serve millions of diverse patients across the UAE and beyond. Our language must be universally understood, whether English is their first language or their third.

DO Speak in plain, conversational English (or MSA Arabic / polished Hindi). Short sentences.
DON'T Use heavy medical jargon unless absolutely necessary — and if we do, explain it immediately.
In practice "High blood pressure" — not "Hypertension" when speaking directly to patients.
04
Proactive
& Forward-Moving

As a hospital-at-home innovator, we don't just treat the immediate symptom — we guide the entire patient journey. Every interaction has a clear next step.

DO Always provide a clear next step or call-to-action. Give the patient a path forward.
DON'T Leave the patient hanging with a dead-end statement or ambiguous outcome.
In practice "If this is a medical emergency, please hang up and dial 998 immediately." — not "We cannot handle emergencies right now."
03 — Brand Matrix

We Are / We Are Not

Four tension pairs that define TruDoc's precise positioning — not what we avoid, but the exact balance we hold at all times.

We Are… But We Are Not… How This Applies to TruDoc Copy
Clinical Experts Cold or Intimidating We back our advice with data, but deliver it with bedside manner. Authority through calmness, never through distance.
Honest Blunt to the Point of Rude We tell the patient the reality of their health, but we cushion it with empathy and a clear treatment plan. Truth + care, always together.
Innovative Gimmicky We are a tech-enabled healthcare platform, but we focus on care — not just the app features. Technology in service of people, not spectacle.
Simple Dumbed Down We explain complex biological or insurance concepts so anyone can understand them — without ever losing accuracy. Plain language is not lazy language.
04 — Tonal Contexts

Voice is Constant.
Tone Adapts.

Our voice never changes. How we express it shifts to match the emotional context of each channel and audience.

📞
IVR / Telehealth App
Calm & Instructional

Patients calling the IVR are anxious. They need structure and reassurance. The tone drops in pace, increases in warmth, and always closes with a concrete next step.

Reassuring Slow-paced Instructional Action-driven
📊
B2B Pitches · Insurance / Capital
Sharp & Visionary

Speaking to Pulsar Capital or insurance partners? Tone pivots to data-driven and executive. Focus on cost-reduction, scale metrics, and the systemic opportunity — not patient stories.

Data-driven Visionary Cost-focused Scalability
🌐
Social Media
Accessible & Proactive

Social is where we build trust before someone ever needs us. The tone is educational, warm, and focused on preventative health — creating a community around wellness, not illness.

Educational Preventative Conversational Proactive

03 — BEHAVIOURAL
How We Act
The Truth-Teller
She doesn't sugarcoat the diagnosis, but always delivers it with a clear, immediate action plan. She tells you what you need — not what costs the most.
The De-Escalator
When a patient is panicking, her energy drops rather than spikes. She absorbs the stress and returns calm, structured guidance.

⚖️
The Truth-Teller

Diagnosis is delivered with honesty — but never without a path forward. She tells you the reality of your health clearly, then immediately hands you the next three steps. No ambiguity. No softening the facts into meaninglessness.

🧘
The De-Escalator

When a situation is critical, her energy inversely drops. She doesn't match panic with panic — she absorbs it. Her steadiness becomes contagious. The anxious caller, the worried parent, the confused patient — all leave the interaction more grounded than when they arrived.

🧭
The Guide

She never creates dead ends. Every conversation, every IVR prompt, every social post closes with a direction. "Here's what happens next. Here's what you do now. Here's who calls you back." Clarity is the most empathetic thing she can offer.
