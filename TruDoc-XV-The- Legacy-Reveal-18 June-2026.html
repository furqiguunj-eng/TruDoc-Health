A physical unboxing, not a notification.

The Save the Date arrives in a laser-cut Mashrabiya sleeve — a gesture before a gathering. The recipient is required to physically lift, slide, and reveal the card within. The unboxing is the first chapter of the evening; the invitation is not received until it is uncovered.

Six hundred sleeves are being produced. Each is hand-finished, individually numbered, and addressed in the recipient's own preferred script.
